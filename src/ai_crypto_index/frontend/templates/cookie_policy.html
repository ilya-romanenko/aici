This Cookie Policy explains how AI-Powered Crypto Index ("Company," "we," "us," and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at http://www.aici.pro or any site that links to this policy. It also describes what these technologies are, why we use them, and how you can control them.

If you have any questions, contact us at contact@aici.pro.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. First-party cookies are set by the website owner (AI-Powered Crypto Index); third-party cookies are set by other providers. Cookies help sites work, measure performance, and remember your preferences.

Why we use cookies

We rely on a minimal set of first-party cookies to keep the AI Crypto Index dashboard and API authentication working. We do not load advertising pixels or third-party analytics cookies by default.

How to control cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings—block third-party cookies, delete stored cookies, or prompt before saving. Blocking our essential cookie may prevent you from signing in or keeping an active session.

Most browsers provide detailed guides on managing cookies, including Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Apple Safari, Microsoft Edge, and Opera. You can also clear cookies at any time to remove stored data.

Other tracking technologies

We may use similar technologies (such as pixels or local storage) to keep the service functional. If they rely on cookies, disabling cookies may affect their operation. We do not deploy third-party advertising trackers.
